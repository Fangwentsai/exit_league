About Us 中文

Established: Spring 2023

"Exit League" is a vibrant darts enthusiast community that combines competitive spirit with friendship. We are dedicated to promoting darts sports culture in Yonghe District, New Taipei City, providing a fair, friendly, and professional competitive platform for players of all levels.

1. League History and Development

Exit League was established in Spring 2023, founded by a group of friends who love darts. We have grown from a few initial teams to become one of the most influential amateur darts leagues in Yonghe District, New Taipei City.

  • Season 1 (2023): League establishment, 6 teams participated, foundation system established
  • Season 2 (Late 2023): Scale expansion, complete scoring system established
  • Season 3 (Early 2024): Data statistics introduced, competitive professionalism enhanced
  • Season 4 (Late 2024): Partner venues increased, community influence expanded
  • Season 5 (2025): Currently ongoing, continuous innovation and growth

3. Our Mission and Vision

Mission: Promote darts sports, build a friendly competitive community, and let more people experience the joy and challenge of darts.

Vision: Become the most distinctive amateur darts league in northern Taiwan, cultivate more darts enthusiasts, and enhance sports culture.

Core Values

  • Fair Competition: Establish transparent competition systems to ensure fairness in every match
  • Friendly Community: Create a warm exchange environment and promote friendship among players
  • Continuous Innovation: Use technology to enhance competition experience and improve data statistics systems
  • Sportsmanship: Cultivate players' sports ethics and teamwork spirit
  • Cultural Promotion: Let more people know and love the sport of darts

4. League Features and Advantages

  • Professional Format: Using international standard 501 format, providing professional competitive experience
  • Data Statistics: Complete recording of each player's performance with detailed data analysis
  • Real-time Updates: Match results uploaded immediately, rankings updated constantly
  • Diverse Events: Regular hosting of different types of competitions and challenges
  • Equipment Support: Partner venues provide quality competition venues and equipment
  • Community Interaction: Online platform established to promote player communication and discussion

5. Competition System and Rules

Our league adopts a complete scoring system to ensure fairness and competitiveness:

  • Season System: Two seasons per year, each lasting about 3-4 months
  • Scoring System: Win/loss points, performance rewards, attendance rewards, and other diverse scoring
  • Ranking Mechanism: Both team and individual rankings emphasized
  • Reward System: Prizes and certificates provided for outstanding performers
  • Promotion/Relegation: Future plans to establish multi-level league system

6. Partner Venue Network

We partner with multiple quality darts venues to provide players with the best competition environment:

  • Exit A: One of the main competition venues in Yonghe District
  • Exit C: Professional darts venue with complete equipment
  • Drunk Vendor: Relaxed atmosphere casual venue
  • Pirate Ship: Themed darts venue with unique features
  • Other Partner Venues: Continuously expanding cooperation network

7. Participation Methods and Entry Requirements

Welcome all darts enthusiasts to join our big family:

Participation Requirements

  • Darts enthusiasts aged 18 and above
  • Willing to follow league rules and sportsmanship
  • Able to regularly participate in competition activities
  • Basic darts skills (beginners welcome)

Joining Process

  • Contact Us: Through email or on-site consultation
  • Trial Experience: Participate in practice matches or friendly games
  • Team Participation: Join existing teams or form new teams
  • Official Registration: Fill out participation information and begin the league journey

8. Community Activities and Exchanges

In addition to formal competitions, we also host various community activities:

  • Practice Matches: Regular informal practice matches

  • Social Gatherings: Enhance friendships among players
