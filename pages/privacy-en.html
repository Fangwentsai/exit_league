Privacy Policy 中文

Last Updated: August 20, 2025

Exit League (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "this website") values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Basic Data: Name, nickname, contact information (email, phone)
  • Competition-related Data: Participation records, game results, statistical data, ranking information
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device information
  • Usage Data: Web browsing records, click behavior, dwell time, access source
  • Preference Settings: Language choice, interface settings, personalization options

2. Data Collection Methods

We collect data through the following methods:

  • Information you actively provide (such as registration, enrollment, contact)
  • Automatically collected technical information (through cookies and similar technologies)
  • Performance data recorded at competition venues
  • Analysis data provided by third-party services

3. Data Usage Purposes

We use the collected data for:

  • League Operations: Event organization, participant management, schedule arrangement
  • Performance Management: Competition records, statistical analysis, ranking calculations
  • Service Provision: Website functionality, user support, technical maintenance
  • Communication: Event notifications, important announcements, responding to inquiries
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns, optimizing user experience, developing new features
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting relevant regulatory requirements, protecting legitimate interests

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data in the following circumstances:

  • Public Competition Information: Participant names, scores, rankings and other competition-related information
  • Service Providers: Technical service providers assisting in website operations (such as cloud services, analytics tools)
  • Legal Requirements: As required by law or government agencies
  • Emergency Situations: Necessary circumstances to protect user or public safety
  • With Your Consent: Other circumstances with your explicit consent

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login status and preference settings
  • Analyze website usage and improve services
  • Provide personalized content and advertising
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraud

You can manage or refuse cookies through browser settings, but this may affect certain website functions.

6. Third-Party Services

This website integrates the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis and user behavior statistics
  • Google AdSense: Advertisement placement and revenue management
  • Google Sheets API: Data storage and synchronization
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting service

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services to understand their data processing methods.

7. Data Security

We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Use HTTPS encryption for transmission
  • Regular backup of important data
  • Restrict data access permissions
  • Monitor abnormal activities
  • Regularly update security measures

8. Data Retention Period

We set retention periods for different types of data:

  • Competition Records: Permanently retained (as historical records)
  • Contact Information: During league period and 2 years after completion
  • Technical Logs: Maximum 1 year retention
  • Advertising Data: According to advertising service provider policies

9. Your Rights

Under relevant laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request to view personal data we hold about you
  • Right of Rectification: Request correction of incorrect or incomplete data
  • Right of Erasure: Request deletion of personal data under specific circumstances
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request restriction of processing certain data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

10. Protection of Minors

This website service is primarily aimed at adults. We do not intentionally collect personal data from children under 13. If such collection is discovered, we will immediately delete the relevant data.
